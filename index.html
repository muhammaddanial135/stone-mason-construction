Who we are

Outdoor Living Space Construction Company

For more than 20 years, Stone Mason Construction has been dedicated to designing and building beautiful outdoor living spaces for homeowners throughout Louisiana and Mississippi. With decades of hands-on experience, our team takes pride in delivering high-quality craftsmanship, thoughtful design, and outdoor spaces built to last.

We specialize in creating custom outdoor environments that bring comfort, style, and functionality to every home. From patios and outdoor kitchens to pergolas, pavilions, and stonework, each project is carefully planned and constructed with attention to detail and a commitment to excellence.

At Stone Mason Construction, we believe your outdoor space should be just as inviting and functional as the inside of your home. That's why we work closely with every client to bring their vision to life while ensuring a smooth, professional building experience from start to finish.
